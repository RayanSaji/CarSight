# CarSight

CarSight is an AI-powered vehicle analysis platform designed to help everyday people make smarter used car buying decisions.

The platform analyzes vehicle listings from private sellers and provides users with insights such as:
- estimated market value
- reliability information
- scam/suspicion indicators
- common issues
- negotiation guidance
- overall deal scoring

The goal of CarSight is to simplify the used car market for people who are inexperienced with cars and help them avoid overpaying or buying unreliable vehicles.

---

## Vision

Buying a used car is overwhelming for most people.

Users often have to:
- browse multiple marketplaces
- compare prices manually
- research reliability on forums
- check KBB values
- ask friends for advice
- guess whether a car is actually worth buying

CarSight centralizes this process into one workflow.

Paste a listing → analyze the vehicle → receive a report.

---

## Core Features (Planned)

### Listing Analyzer
Analyze pasted vehicle listings or manually entered car information.

### Deal Score Engine
Determine whether a vehicle is:
- underpriced
- fairly priced
- overpriced

### Reliability Engine
Provide reliability insights using:
- known issues
- common failures
- recall information
- ownership risk data

### Confidence / Risk Rating
Detect suspicious or potentially misleading listings.

### Negotiation Assistant
Provide talking points and offer suggestions based on market data and known vehicle issues.

## Product Philosophy

CarSight is designed to be:
- simple
- informative
- beginner-friendly
- transparent
- fast

The platform should simplify complex car information instead of overwhelming users with technical jargon.
